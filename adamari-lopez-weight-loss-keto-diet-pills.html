Adamari Lopez's Keto Diet Legit Or Scam?

Adamari Lopez was the first Latina ambassador of the eating plan over two years ago, featured in Oprah Winfrey WW’s Weight Loss and Healthy Living Program and through that she achieved a healthy lifestyle by dropping some few pounds. However, her weight loss was associated with Keto diet pills and started popping up on advertisements for the pills. Therefore, the actress broke her silence after two years and explained that she worked hard and kept up with her motivation to lose over 20 lbs. She even left soft drinks from her diet and sticked with the program as much as she could.

She further added that her family has never created the conscience of fat or skinny but of a healthy lifestyle and said, “WW is there to help us, but it does not work miracles. You are the one who must do your part. The road is going to seem long and difficult, but it is not impossible. Bet on you. Remember that life is beautiful.”

The actress has never endorsed any specific keto diet pills and isn’t a part of it unless she says it directly in future.

The ingredients in Adamari Lopez keto diet are BHB Ketone, Garcinia Cambogia and Apple Cider Vinegar. BHB Ketone uses the fat cells for energy production and allows us to get energetic while losing healthy weight. Garcinia Cambogia stimulates metabolic rate and suppresses the appetite levels to augment the weight loss while Apple Cider Vinegar protects the cells from damages and comes with many medical benefits.

The daily dose of Adamari Lopez Keto is two capsules per day with water according to the instructions on label. It suggest the user to take the doses at least 30 minutes before workout and consume it regularly for 2-3 months.

The Adamari Lopez Keto has been gaining recognition for being the first natural and safe product for weight loss. But every formula can react differently to everybody which means the user should follow the instructions strictly to avoid any side effects from the formula. However, till today no side effects have been reported.
