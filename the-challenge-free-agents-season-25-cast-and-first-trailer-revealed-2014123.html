The Challenge: Free Agents Season 25 Cast and First Trailer Revealed

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 13, 2024

Drama, hookups, veterans, and rookies! The next installment of MTV’s The Challenge has been announced, but with a huge twist for the cast. In the show’s 25th season, the contestants will be playing an individual game in The Challenge: Free Agents for a chance to win over $350,000 in prize money. 

BMX rider T.J. Lavin returns to host as 28 players head to Punta del Este, Uruguay, to battle it out in terrifying competitions — all the while making and breaking alliances. (Oh, and CT’s scruff returns, but ex Diem Brown doesn’t.)
