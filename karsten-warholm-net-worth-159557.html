Karsten Warholm is a Norwegian runner who was born on February 28, 1996 in Ulsteinvik, Norway. He is renowned for his success in the 400 meter sprint and hurdles events, having won a gold medal in the 400 meter hurdles at the 2017 World Championships and European U-23 Championships. Warholm is a highly accomplished athlete and continues to be a force to be reckoned with in the world of track and field.
