Ford made the 2600 Utility tractor with a gasoline and diesel engine. Both engines have a 7-quart oil capacity.

Both engines have three cylinders and are rated at 2000 rpm. The transmission system has 8 forward and 2 reverse gears.

Serial numbers for 2600 are located on the right side of the ford 2600 tractor behind the starter. Ford 2600 Utility tractor has 2WD, power-assist steering with differential mechanical expanding shoe brakes
