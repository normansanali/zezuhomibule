Jessica Szohr Career and Professional Life

Jessica initially desired a career as an interior designer and enrolled in Columbia College Chicago. However, her agent persuaded her to audition for pilot season. As a result, she made her acting debut in 2003, in an episode of My Wife and Kids’ third season titled “My Wife and Kids: Not So Hostile Takeover.” In the same year, she starred in her first film, Uncle Nino, as The MC.

She then appeared as a guest star on numerous television shows, including That’s So Raven, Drake & Josh, What I Like About You, and Joan of Arcadia. Jessica has made cameo appearances in the films Somebody Help Me, The Reading Room, and Fired Up!

She starred as Kelly in the horror film Piranha 3D and was featured in a Funny or Die promotional video alongside the film’s star cast. In April 2010, she joined the cast of Mandy Moore and Kellan Lutz’s romantic comedy Love, Wedding, Marriage.

In 2012, she landed the role of Orange in Hirokin: The Last Samurai, an independent post-apocalyptic sci-fi film. Her portrayal later became known as a “cunning temptress.”

Jessica appeared in Taylor Swift’s music video for the song 22 in 2013.
