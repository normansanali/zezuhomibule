Who is Kishor Bajaj

Kishor was a well-known Indian entrepreneur, investor, and philanthropist who made significant contributions to the industries of hospitality, real estate, and healthcare. He was born in Bombay, India, in 1959, and graduated from the University of Mumbai with a bachelor’s degree in business. Kishor Bajaj began his career in the hotel sector in 1983, when he formed the Badasaab Group, afterward renamed the Bajaj Group. The Bajaj Group has developed to become one of India’s premier hospitality and real estate enterprises, with a portfolio of luxury hotels, resorts, and residential complexes, under his guidance.

Kishor was widely considered a pioneer of India’s fine dining business, and he was instrumental in establishing Mumbai’s reputation as a gourmet hotspot. He was active in the healthcare business, investing in and establishing various hospitals and clinics around India to provide quality treatment to the disadvantaged.
