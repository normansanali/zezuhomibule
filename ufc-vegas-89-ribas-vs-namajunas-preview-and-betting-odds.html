UFC Vegas 89: Ribas vs. Namajunas Preview and Betting Odds

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, September 2, 2024

The highly anticipated UFC Vegas 89 event is set to take place on Saturday, March 23, 2024, at the UFC Apex in Las Vegas, Nevada. The main event will feature a compelling showdown between former two-time Strawweight champion, Rose Namajunas, and Flyweight contender, Amanda Ribas. Alongside this thrilling matchup, the event will also showcase other exciting fights on the main card.

Main Event: Namajunas vs. Ribas

In the main event, Rose Namajunas, listed at -180 at DraftKings Sportsbook, will face Amanda Ribas, who is listed as the +150 underdog. Namajunas, a two-time UFC Strawweight champion, aims to secure her first win at 125 pounds after suffering two consecutive losses. On the other hand, Ribas seeks to solidify her position in the Flyweight division after bouncing between 115- and 125-pounds. The betting odds favor Namajunas, with the prediction leaning towards a victory for Namajunas via knockout [+140]1.

Co-Main Event and Other Fights

The co-main event will feature a Heavyweight tussle between Karl Williams and Justin Tafa, with Williams listed as the favorite at -180. Additionally, Edmen Shahbazyan will seek redemption against A.J. Dobson in the Middleweight division, with Shahbazyan favored at -192. Moreover, Payton Talbott will face Cameron Saaiman in a Bantamweight matchup, with Saaiman surprisingly listed as the underdog at +124. The Featherweight bout between Billy Quarantillo and Youssef Zalal also promises an intense showdown, with Quarantillo being the favorite at -162.

Betting Odds for Additional Fights

  • Edmen Shahbazyan (-180) vs. A.J. Dobson (+150)
  • Payton Talbott (-148) vs. Cameron Saaiman (+124)
  • Billy Quarantillo (-162) vs. Youssef Zalal (+136)

The event is expected to deliver an action-packed fight card with a mix of experienced fighters and rising talents from different weight divisions. Fans and bettors alike can anticipate an electrifying evening of MMA action as these formidable athletes step into the Octagon.

For those looking to place their bets, DraftKings Sportsbook offers betting opportunities for the UFC Vegas 89 event, providing a comprehensive platform for wagering on the thrilling matchups and potential outcomes.

This event presents a remarkable opportunity for both fighters to showcase their skills and make a statement in their respective divisions. UFC Vegas 89 is set to captivate MMA enthusiasts with its compelling matchups and promise of high-stakes competition.
