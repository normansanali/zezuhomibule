Stanley cups are everywhere this summer. They come in super cute colors, a variety of sizes, and keep your drinks cold throughout the unrelenting heat — but there’s just one downside. The open-straw concept can let flies in (been there!), spill, or get contaminated by germs if someone touches it. The solution? Silicone straw toppers! Amazon recently released a bunch of cute ones for less than $8, and we’ve never clicked “add to cart” so fast!

One option is totally summery. The Eight-Piece Straw Cover Caps for Stanley Cups fit on 30-ounce and 40-ounce tumblers and come in eight colorful shapes, including a kitty paw, clouds and a strawberry. They’re still on sale for post-Prime Day for less than $9, which means they’re a little over $1 a piece!
