Fisk Season 3: Cast, Plot, and More

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Fisk Season 3: The dryly hilarious, quintessentially Australian legal dramedy “Fisk” has had fans in stitches since its 2021 debut. The show, created by and starring the inimitable Kitty Flanagan as the sharp-tongued, brown-suit-clad lawyer Helen Tudor-Fisk, has garnered critical acclaim and awards recognition, leaving viewers desperate for more. But will there be a “Fisk” Season 3? Buckle up, cobblers, because we’re diving deep into the murky waters of speculation and rumour to answer that very question.

Is Fisk Season 3 Happening?

The answer, unfortunately, is as clear as Melbourne mud on a rainy day – maybe. While “Fisk” Season 3 hasn’t been officially confirmed by the Australian Broadcasting Corporation (ABC), there are several promising signs.

  • Renewal Buzz: In November 2021, ABC commissioned a third season even before Season 2 aired. This indicates confidence in the show’s performance and a desire to continue Helen’s legal (and comedic) adventures.
  • Cast Optimism: Julia Zemiro, who plays Roz Gruber, Helen’s colleague and confidante, expressed hope for Season 3, stating that it all hinges on Kitty Flanagan and her sister Penny (the show’s co-writer) finishing the script.
  • Critical Acclaim: “Fisk” has raked in awards, including the prestigious Logie Award for Most Outstanding Comedy Program and Flanagan’s AACTA win for Best Lead Actress in a Comedy Series. Such recognition bodes well for the show’s future.

However, there are also reasons for cautious optimism. Flanagan is a busy woman, juggling stand-up comedy tours with writing duties. Additionally, Season 2’s finale wrapped up several storylines in a satisfying way, potentially making it a natural endpoint for the series.

Ultimately, the decision to renew “Fisk” rests with the ABC. Until then, all we can do is cross our fingers, channel our inner Roz, and declare, “Bring on the brown suits, the wills and estates, and the questionable life choices – we’re ready for more Fisk!”

Fisk Season 3 Cast

The heart and soul of “Fisk” lies in its stellar cast. So, can we expect the gang to return for another round of legal shenanigans?

  • Kitty Flanagan as Helen Tudor-Fisk: The show’s anchor, Helen’s dry wit and relatable messiness are irreplaceable. Rest assured, Flanagan will be back if there’s a Season 3.
  • Marty Sheargold as Ray Gruber: The gruff but loveable boss with a soft spot for Helen, Ray’s presence is essential to the show’s dynamic. Sheargold’s return is highly likely.
  • Julia Zemiro as Roz Gruber: Ray’s sister and Helen’s confidante, Roz brings warmth and humour to the legal proceedings. We can safely assume Zemiro will be back to dispense sassy advice and questionable life choices.
  • Aaron Chen as George: The ever-helpful paralegal/receptionist/IT whiz, George is a scene-stealer with his deadpan delivery and quirky talents. Chen’s return is almost guaranteed.

Of course, the return of other beloved characters like Gina Riley’s formidable Judge Brewster and Roz’s on-again-off-again love interest, the enigmatic Dr. Fong, remains to be confirmed. But one thing’s for sure – the “Fisk” crew is as dysfunctional and delightful as ever, and their return would be a cause for celebration.

Plot of Fisk Season 3

Predicting the plot of “Fisk” is like trying to wrangle a rogue emu – unpredictable and often hilarious. However, based on Season 2’s finale, here are some potential plotlines for Season 3:

  • Helen’s New Life: Having finally made partner, Helen faces the challenges of leadership and the ever-present temptation to revert to her old ways.
  • Unfinished Business: Some loose ends remain from Season 2, like the fate of Helen’s estranged brother and the ongoing saga of Roz’s love life. These could provide fuel for future comedic fires.
