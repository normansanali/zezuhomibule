Christina Haack Is a Mom of Three Kids on Top of Her Busy HGTV Schedule

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, September 11, 2024

Christina Haack has three kids from her previous marriages to Tarek El Moussa and Ant Anstead. Learn more about her relationships and children.

Real estate investor and media personality Christina Haack has been featured in a number of HGTV shows. Although Flip or Flop — the series on which she starred with her ex-husband, Tarek El Moussa — was officially canceled in March 2022, she still has a solo project called Christina on the Coast. And coming in 2023 is a spinoff titled Christina in the Country.

On top of her busy work schedule, Christina is also a mom. She frequently takes to Instagram to share updates about her three kids: Taylor and Brayden El Moussa, and Hudson Anstead. Let's take a closer look at her children and current relationship status.

Christina Haack shares two kids with her first husband, Tarek El Moussa.

From her first marriage with Tarek came two kids, Taylor (born in 2010) and Brayden (born in 2015). Christina and Tarek were married for nine years before splitting in 2016; their divorce was finalized in 2018.

It's understood that exes and Flip or Flop co-stars Christina and Tarek are big on co-parenting. Christina previously told People that she and Tarek were a "united front." Even though they both went on to date other people after the marriage fell apart, Christina and Tarek have insisted on maintaining a kid-centric approach, always prioritizing the little ones.

Tarek married Heather Rae Young in October 2021.

Chrisina Haack also shares a son with her second husband, Ant Anstead.

Shortly after she and Tarek were officially divorced, Christina went public with her relationship with Ant Anstead. They married in late 2018 and welcomed a son, Hudson London, in 2019.

Unfortunately, their marriage wasn't a lasting one. Christina and Ant announced their separation in September 2020 and finalized their divorce in 2021. In the divorce, Christina got to keep five houses and several cars, including a Range Rover and a Bentley. She also kept her wedding ring, per Entertainment Tonight. She and Ant were initially awarded joint legal and physical custody of Hudson, the outlet states. However, Ant filed for full custody in April 2022.

Christina made public her relationship with Joshua Hall in the summer of 2021. Meanwhile, Ant has been seen with Renée Zellweger.

Christina Haack married Josh Hall in 2022 — is she pregnant again?

Christina and Josh quietly tied the knot sometime in early 2022 or late 2021, TMZ reported in April 2022. According to the publication, her name was changed to Christina Hall on her real estate license. So far, neither Christina nor Josh have announced anything about a pregnancy — but we'll keep you updated if that changes!

Josh is a real estate agent who previously worked for the Foundry Group and Spyglass Realty in Texas before joining Christina to form Unbroken Productions. Fun fact: his sisters, Stacie Adams and Jessica Hall, appeared on The Hills.

And soon, Josh will get a taste of reality TV for himself when his and Christina's new show, Christina in the Country, premieres on Jan. 12, 2023 on HGTV. Catch new episodes of Christina on the Coast Thursdays at 9 p.m. EST on HGTV.
