Sumya Anani – Professional Life and Career

Sumya Anani began boxing as a way of self-defense due to a lack of funds before progressing to professional boxing. She took part in weightlifting in 1995 and 1996. She began professional boxing in 1996, but owing to a lack of funds, she was forced to fight primarily unknown opponents.

However, she made her professional debut at the International Women’s Boxing Hall of Fame in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, in 2016.
