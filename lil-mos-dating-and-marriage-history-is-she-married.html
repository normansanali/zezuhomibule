Lil Mos Dating And Marriage History: Is She Married?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, September 12, 2024

American radio host Cynthia Loving, popularly known as Lil Mo is also an R&B performer, record producer, songwriter, and member of the group “Hot Boyz.” She was born in a military family on November 19, 1975.

She spent the majority of her childhood on Long Island, New York, but frequently relocated due to her father’s military service and various deployments.

Cynthia and Jacob Loving are Lil Mo’s parents. They moved about a lot during Lil Mo’s upbringing, living in places including Texas, Georgia, and North Carolina before settling in Baltimore, Maryland.
Lil Mo had a young dream of becoming a well-known singer.

As a result, she put in a lot of effort to achieve her dream, participating in numerous talent competitions to display her distinctive voice whenever her family could stay put for an extended period of time.

Lil Mo Personal Life/ Dating History

In a Washington, DC, gas station in 2001, Lil Mo first met her husband, Al Stone. She started the conversation by requesting his phone number.

He proposed to her in the same year, and she said yes. After three months, on August 29, 2001, they were wed. Only five months were spent together. Together, the couple gave birth to two daughters: God’Iss Love Stone was born on February 24, 2005, and Heaven Love’on Stone was born on August 19, 2002.

Ten months after the birth of her second daughter, in December 2005, she filed for divorce from her husband, Al. After that, in 2009, she got married to Gospel musician Philip Bryant.

Lil Mo’s Dating History

Lil Mo remarried in 2019 after divorcing her first husband Al in December 2005, 10 months after the birth of her second daughter.

She married Gospel musician Philip Bryant. Philip and Lil Mo have two boys.
Their youngest son, Jonah Maddox-Phillip Bryant, was born on July 10, 2012, and their first son, Justin McKenzie Phillip, was born on January 16, 2009. Lil Mo and Phillip’s marriage was troubled from the beginning.
The marriage was doomed to end due to allegations of infidelity and overall unhappiness. In the end, they parted ways in 2014. Just before she quit her relationship with Philip, Lil Mo had an abortion.

Does Lil Mo have a boyfriend or a husband?

Lil Mo claimed that Philip, her ex- husband was attempting to confine her inside the home and use any measures necessary to get her pregnant. She had no intention of getting married again after their divorce and had intended to remain alone for some time. But that same year, she revealed to the world that she was now dating professional boxer Karl Dargan.

Lil Mo revealed she was unmarried for a long time before meeting Karl, despite having earlier declared she only wanted to remain single after her marriage to Phillip ended. She said that before divorcing Philip, she had been living apart from him. Even though she was aware that there was no chance for reconciliation, she persisted out of concern for her children.
