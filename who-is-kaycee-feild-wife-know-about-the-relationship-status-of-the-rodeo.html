Who Is Kaycee Feild Wife? Know About The Relationship Status Of The Rodeo

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 5, 2024

Kaycee Feild, the five-time winner of the Bareback Riding World Championship, has reached another NFR Finals after being a year away from the sport due to the pandemic.

As an American professional rodeo cowboy, Kaycee Feild has previously won four Wrangler National Finals Rodeo titles. He also has a record of being the first Cowboy to secure three consecutive bareback riding averages titles. 

Stephanie Feild, the wife of Kaycee, was seen cheering for her husband from the stands along with her three children. Kaycee is in contention for winning yet another NFR Title.

Kaycee Feild Wife: Who Is He Married To?

Kaycee Feild got married to Stephanie Calentine on July 31, 2012. The couple shares their home with two daughters Chaimberlyn Dawn and Remington Kay and Huxyn Lewis’s son. 

Stephanie Feild is an Instagram Model and an influencer with almost 20k followers. She is often seen posting about her husband’s achievements and personal life. She is also engaged in promoting different brands through her social sites. 

The couple has been married for nine years and seems happier than ever. Kaycee’s wife and children are often seen cheering for their favorite bareback rider in the stands. 

Stephanie often refers to her husband as “My Cowboy,” which signifies strong support for her husband’s career. So far, there have been no rumors regarding any problems in the couple’s married life.

Kaycee Feild Age: How Old Is He?

Kaycee Feild is currently 34 years old and actively participates in world championships. Feild was born in Payson, Utah, on March 4, 1987. 

Kaycee began practicing bareback riding broncs at the age of 13. However, his father was afraid that he would get hurt, due to which young Feild wasn’t restricted from practice until he was 17 years old. 

Feild won his first amateur National Intercollegiate Rodeo Association bareback title in 2008. Kaycee won his first title at the age of 25 and announced his arrival at the PRCA.

Kaycee Feild Wikipedia  

Kaycee Feild is the son of five-time all-around rodeo champion late Lewis Feild. He followed in his father’s footsteps to become one of the greatest rodeo cowboys of all time. Kaycee joined Professional Rodeo Cowboys Association (PRCA) in 2007 and hasn’t looked back in his life ever since. 

Kaycee attended Utah Valley University, where his father worked as a rodeo coach. The Rodeo idolizes his father and credits him for his successful career as a bareback rider. 

Feild won his first PRCA bareback riding title and the world title in 2011 and won consecutive NFR Wrangler titles for 2012-2014.

Since his debut in bareback riding, Kaycee has been smashing every record possible in his name. Currently, he has tied with bareback riding legends Bruce Ford and Joe Alexander for most PRCA bareback riding World Championship titles.
