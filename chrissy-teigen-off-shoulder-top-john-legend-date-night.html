Chrissy Teigen and John Legend Have Dinner Date in L.A.

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Does Chrissy Teigen ever have an off day?

The model-turned-cookbook author stepped out for an intimate dinner date with her husband John Legend in West Hollywood, Calif., last night, and the 30-year-old wowed in an eye-catching look. For the occasion, Teigen opted for a white off-the-shoulder top that accentuated her curves and breezy wide-leg mustard yellow trousers that elongated her gams. She completed her ensemble with simple nude heeled sandals, minimal jewelry, and a chic braided high ponytail. For an extra pop of color, the beauty swiped on a bold berry lip color that we're completely obsessed with. Meanwhile, her husband looked dapper in a monochrome outfit that consisted of a black-and-white floral print collared shirt, tailored black pants, a coordinating leather jacket, and fresh white kicks.

The new parents were all smiles as they made their way out of the restaurant hand-in-hand after their romantic meal, and we can't help but marvel over the fact that Teigen had a baby just a mere four months ago.

Talk about one stylish night on the town.
