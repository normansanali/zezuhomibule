Amir Wilson Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Who is Amir Wilson?

Amir Wilson is an English actor known for his role as Tiuri in the Netflix series The Letter for the King. Furthermore, he is known for his appearance as Will Parry in the HBO and BBC One series His Dark Materials.

What are the birth facts of Amir Wilson?

Amir Wilson was born on 6 February 2004, in Shrewsbury, Shropshire, England. His birth name is Amir Ahmad Wilson.

Amir’s mother Nagla is Sudanese and his father Paul is English. His father was an employee at the bank and his mother was a teacher. Likewise, he has one sibling, an elder sister named Iman. At the age of 10, he moved to London to stay with his sister and grew up in the suburb of Monkmoor.

Amir Wilson is of British nationality and his ethnicity is mixed (English, Sudanese). Furthermore, he attended St. Giles’ C of E Primary School. Later, he trained at the local theatre group, Get Your Wigle On.

Career, Professional Life

During Amir Wilson’s collegiate days, he has taken part in many plays, acts, and stage shows. His career started when he got a chance to appear as Young Simba in The Lion King at Lyceum Theatre, London.

Later, he went on to do Nigel in the stage performance, The Secret Diary of Adrian Mole Aged 13¾. He made his film debut in 2018 with Special Delivery as Josh. The next year, he had a brief role in The Kid Who Would Be King followed by the main role in His Dark Materials same year.

In 2020, he appeared as Tiuri in the Netflix series The Letter for the King and as Dickon in The Secret Garden. Recently, he made an appearance as Anton Milanesi in The Magic Flute.

Is Amir Wilson active on social media?

Amir is highly active on social media like Twitter and Instagram. He has over 5.7k followers on Twitter and over 36.6k followers on Instagram.

Awards, Nominations

He was nominated for Daytime Emmy Awards in 2021 under the category Younger Performer in a Daytime Fiction Program for his work in The Letter for the King.

Salary, Net Worth, Income

Amir Wilson has an estimated net worth of around $1 million and has earned hefty from his profession.
