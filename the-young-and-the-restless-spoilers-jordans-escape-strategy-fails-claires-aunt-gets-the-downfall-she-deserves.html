The Young and the Restless Spoilers: Jordans Escape Strategy Fails Claires Aunt Gets the D

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 17, 2024

The Young and the Restless (Y&R) spoilers reveal that Aunt Jordan’s (Colleen Zenk) sneaky move won’t help her avoid justice for long. Jordan needs to go down for the horrific scheme she set in motion and all the pain she’s inflicted on the Newman clan, so Y&R will thankfully back her into a corner soon.

In the November 29 episode, Claire Grace (Hayley Erin) got arrested while Jordan hid in the house to prevent her own capture and peeked through the vent once she was alone.

Although Claire may initially stick to her claim that she has no idea where Jordan could be, she must have some notion of what Jordan planned to do after this plot played out.

Jordan surely didn’t think she could target this many high-profile Newmans and sweep it under the rug! This is the kind of thing that could cause media chaos and launch a massive news story.

It seems likely Jordan had some sort of escape strategy in place long before she got Claire to bring Nikki Newman (Melody Thomas Scott) and the others to the lake house.

After all, Jordan has had decades to get ready for her revenge! It’d be rather foolish if Jordan didn’t plan a quick getaway for herself once her crimes were committed.

Maybe Jordan always planned to hide in the house somewhere if things went sideways – at least until the coast was clear.

In the real world, it seems like the cops would do a more thorough search and stay posted at the crime scene, but the house may be left unattended for soapy reasons.

Jordan might have a plane or getaway car waiting for her somewhere, but maybe she’ll get intercepted as she tries to flee.

Although Jordan will hope she can avoid punishment, that doesn’t seem likely since Colleen Zenk, Hayley Erin and Christian LeBlanc (Michael Baldwin) all snapped a photo together on a set that pointed to some jailhouse drama.

It appears Jordan will be joining Claire in police custody soon, so will Claire betray her aunt again and perhaps tell the cops where to look for her?

Claire might know where Jordan originally planned to go after the scheme wrapped up, so she could perhaps cut a deal. There might even be some situation where Claire serves as bait to lure Jordan out of hiding and into a trap!

The Young and the Restless spoilers hint that Jordan’s going down one way or another, so stick with us for updates on her imminent time behind bars.
