Who is Edin Hasanovic?

Edin Hasanovic is an actor of German-Bosnian nationality who was seen in both TV plus films. Edin Hasanovic is popular for featuring in Shifting the Blame (2012), Familie Braun (2016), Herbert (2015), etc.

Recently in 2022, he was cast as Tjaden Stackfleet in a war-novel movie All Quiet on the Western Front.

He was specially thanked for his contribution to 2011’s short movie Summer Knows. As a host, along with Christine Westermann, he is associated with the podcast Jung und Jiinger.

Edin Hasanovic- Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

His birthdate is 02 April 1992 and the place he was born in is Zvornik, Bosnia, and Herzegovina. The name of his mother is unknown along with his father’s identity.

But his mom ran from Bosnia to Germany after few months of his birth.

Further, Edin’s ancestry is Bosnian, and as to his academics, he passed out from Heinrich Schliemann High School in 2011.
