Did Derrick Ward Play in the NFL?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Did Derrick Ward Play in the NFL? Derrick LaRon Ward, a former American football running back, made a name for himself in the National Football League (NFL) during his career. This article aims to delve into Ward's NFL journey, highlighting his teams, accomplishments, and his eventual retirement.

Early Life and College Career

Derrick Ward was born on August 30, 1980. He played college football at Fresno State University and Ottawa University. Ward showcased his talent and potential during his college career, which eventually led him to the NFL.

NFL Career

New York Jets (2004-2007) Derrick Ward's NFL journey began when he was drafted by the New York Jets. He played for the Jets from 2004 to 2007, contributing to the team's offense as a running back.

New York Giants (2008-2009) In 2007, Ward signed with the New York Giants as a free agent. He had a remarkable stint with the Giants, playing a crucial role in their Super Bowl victory in 2008. Ward's contributions to the team's success solidified his status as a talented running back in the NFL.

Tampa Bay Buccaneers (2009-2010) After his successful tenure with the Giants, Ward joined the Tampa Bay Buccaneers in 2009. He continued to showcase his skills on the field, contributing to the Buccaneers' offense. Ward's versatility and reliability as a running back made him a valuable asset for the team.

Houston Texans (2011-2012) Ward's final stop in the NFL was with the Houston Texans. He played for the Texans in the 2011 and 2012 seasons, providing depth and experience to the team's running back corps. Ward's presence and knowledge of the game were highly regarded by his teammates and coaches.

Accomplishments and Statistics

Throughout his NFL career, Derrick Ward achieved several notable accomplishments. He rushed for over 2,600 yards and scored 12 touchdowns. These numbers reflect his skill and effectiveness as a running back in the league. Ward's contributions to the New York Giants' Super Bowl victory in 2008 also stand as a significant milestone in his career.

Retirement and Recent Events

After the 2012 season, Derrick Ward decided to retire from professional football. Following his retirement, he remained involved in the football community, offering his expertise and insights to aspiring athletes.

However, in a surprising turn of events, Ward was arrested in 2023 on suspicion of robbery. The details surrounding the arrest remain undisclosed, but it marked a stark contrast to his successful NFL career. The incident shocked many fans and followers of Ward's journey.
