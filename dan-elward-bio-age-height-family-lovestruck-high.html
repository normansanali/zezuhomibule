Who Is Lovestruck High's Dan Elward? His Bio, Age, Height, Family

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 11, 2024

Dan Elward sailed through his high school in the “popular gang.” He would “walk around with a bag on one shoulder and (his) Justin Bieber haircut — with a group of nine girlfriends and him.” However, unlike now, he confessed to being “fat and ugly” then. So, getting a chance to go back to high school on Amazon’s dating show Lovestruck High, Dan was confident that he’ll find love and also “end up being friends with all the girls again.”

Get to know him better as this Dan Elward Bio proceeds.

Dan Elward On Lovestruck High

Amazon’s new dating show Lovestruck High premiered on May 18, 2022, and it featured 17 participants including Dan Elward all set to experience high school life and attend classes like any other students but in hopes of finding love.

Furthermore, the participants were also provided with a principal (Ria Lina), a coach (Russell Hicks), and a homeroom teacher (Miss Kelly – Chloe Zeitounian) who guided them and entertained them with different tasks. Also, it was up to them to expel the “students” if they ever put a foot wrong.

And to make the show even more interesting, the story was narrated by actress Lindsay Lohan who’s known for her roles in Mean Girls, Freaky Friday, and Confessions of a Teen Drama Queen.

Joining Dan for this inaugural season were her co-stars — Ada, Alex, Basit, Chante, Charlie, Geoff, Huss, Jeff, Jodi, Junaid, KT, Max, Megan, Sin, Theo, and Yasmine.

All of them ranged from 20 – 30 years of age.

Excited to let his followers know he was one of the participants, Dan took it to his IG on April 21, 2022, to share, “The secret is FINALLY out catch me on @primevideouk @lovestruckhigh dropping May 18th.”

Did you know: Dan’s ideal man is so someone who has similar ways that they can have fun with. Or someone like, Ben Fogle, “I think it is his big nose. I have got a big nose but his is huge,” he joked.

Is Dan Elward Dating Anyone?

Dan Elward didn’t seem like he was dating anyone as of 2022.

And for the ones who’ve been following Dan over his socials, and seeing the beautiful girl Emerlee Brown often beside him, she isn’t his partner. Dan’s gay! But then again, Emerlee’s IG captions such as labeling Dan her man or duo were certainly misleading.

Also, Emerlee was already in a relationship with the owner of Bridgend Carpentry Co, Elliot Antony Spear.

Here’s Emerlee’s IG @emerleebrown and Fb @emerlee.brown/

Now talking about Dan’s romantic interest in Lovestruck High, he was smitten by Junaid the moment he laid his eyes on him. The two were captured heavily flirting before the principal Nelson announced that the contestants did not have a lot of time to find a partner for the homecoming dance.

Naturally, Dan was then asked to the dance by Junaid, and they made it to the ball as a pair.

Later, the duo also bonded over a couple’s painting session, and it was lovely to see the love and adoration they had for each other. Having a genuine connection, the pair seemed like they might just be the homecoming king and queen. But sadly, in ep 3, Dan seemingly got quite interested in the newcomer, Basit, and he even went on to share a kiss with him during a CPR training session.

So, that was the end for Dan and Junaid, Though the two couldn’t pursue a romantic relationship, they still were good friends at the time of writing this article.

In case you’re interested in Junaid, he is a model crowned as the “Selfie King.” Before Lovestruck High, he was popular for his appearance on the Channel 4 series Obsessed with My Body, mostly for his obsessive grooming habits.
