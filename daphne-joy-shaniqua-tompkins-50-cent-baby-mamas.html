Meet 50 Cent's Baby Mamas

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 19, 2024
  • 50 Cent’s baby mamas are Podcast host, Shaniqua Tompkins, and model, Daphne Joy
  • The rapper has two children Marquise Jackson and Sire Jackson from his 2 baby mamas
  • He recently trolled Daphne Joy, his youngest child’s mother for hanging out with rapper Sean Combs popularly known as P Diddy

50 Cent’s baby mamas are Shaniqua Tompkins and Daphne Joy. He has two children, Marquise Jackson, and Sire Jackson. The rapper’s real name is Curtis James Jackson, he rose to fame in the 90s. He is an American rapper, actor, and TV producer.

The rapper is famous for having messy breakups with his baby mamas. Recently, his son Marquise Jackson called him out for not providing enough money for his upkeep.

50 Cent Baby Mama, Daphne Joy Cervantes Narvaez is a Puerto Rican model, actor, entrepreneur, and TV host. She is the mother of 50 Cent’s youngest son, Sire Jackson.

The Puerto-Rican model was born in Olongapo, Philippines, on February 8, 1987, to a Philipino mother and a Puerto-Rican father. She grew up in California after her family moved to the US when she was 7years old.

Daphne fell in love with modeling as a child. However, she didn’t chase it as a career until she was 17 years old. As a professional model, Daphne has appeared on many magazine covers including Show Magazine, King Magazine, BlackMen Magazine, Smoooth, and Ed Hardy Swim Campaign.

The Philippine-born actress owns an online boutique, Daphne Joy Collection, where she sells different clothing items, swimwear, purses, and other accessories.

50 Cents baby mama is passionate about helping children, she uses her social media platforms to raise awareness for child abuse. The model is affiliated with Child Rescue Coalition, an organization that collaborates with global forces to rescue abused children and apprehend abusers.

As an actor, Daphne has featured in several movies, including “Pirates of the Caribbean” (2011), “Setup” (2011), and “Frankenhood” (2009).

Daphne Joy’s Relationship with Rapper 50 Cent

The mother of one was in a 2-year relationship with Rapper, 50 Cent. However, the relationship ended after Daphne accused the rapper of domestic assault and destroying her property in Toluca Lake.

Although the charges were later dropped, the relationship ended on bad terms but they remain cordial for the sake of their child.

On September 24, 2022, Daphne posted a video of herself during P Diddy’s performance. Following her post, 50 Cent posted a picture of himself and their son, Sire, with the caption

“Oh Sh*t, that’s your mommy over there with Puffy. LOL. Remember what I told you the other day, these b****es be crazy.

Daphne responded with a post on her Instagram on how tired she is of defending her character and requested that the rapper stops bothering her, as she doesn’t bother him.

The model has also been in a relationship with singer, Jason Derulo. However, their relationship ended after 7 months.

Born on September 17, 1975, in Queens, New York, Shaniqua Tompkins is a reality TV star famous for being one of Rapper 50 Cent’s baby mamas. The duo was in a relationship between 1994-1998.

Shaniqua is a co-host of a podcast show called “2 Queens from Queen”, which she hosted alongside the Ex-wife of a former basketball player, Lamar Odom, Liza Morales. The show advocates women’s issues and reinforces women of every color.

During the duration of their relationship, the couple welcomed a son Marquise Jackson on October 13, 1996, in Los Angeles, California. Their son has also grown to be a rapper like his father, who calls himself 25 Cents.

Relationship with Rapper, 50 Cents 

50 Cents is known for his messy breakups with his baby mamas. The drama between the duo is insane. There has been bad blood between them since their breakup over 20 years ago.

After their relationship turned bitter, Shaniqua filed a $50 million lawsuit against the rapper, but the court dismissed the case.

They were also in a lengthy custody battle, which ended in 2008 when their son Marquise was 12 years old. After years of dragging each other in court, the duo finally came to an agreement, and the rapper was granted visitation rights to his son. He was allowed to have the boy for one weekend every month.

Despite being apart for over 2 decades, Shaniqua and her baby daddy are constantly at each other’s throats and that relationship has transcended down to their son.

Marquise and his father, 50 Cents do not get along well. Recently the boy called out his father for not providing him with enough money to keep up with his lifestyle. This was after he acknowledged that he got $6,700 from his father monthly for upkeep.

50 Cent is not the first American rapper to have more than one baby mama. There are other famous people who have many baby mamas including Nick Canon, who has about 9 children with different women.
