Joe Rogan: Fans react to funny meme of commentator 'doing his job' as Joe Biden falls on ground

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, September 7, 2024

Joe Rogan has been a part of the UFC for almost two decades, both as a commentator and as an interviewer. His role as the in-cage interviewer has resulted in innumerable memes depicting him "interviewing" people in embarrassing settings.

A meme of Rogan interviewing Biden was created after US President Joe Biden's terrible fall at a US Air Force Academy ceremony. People are reacting to Biden's fall, with some memes and others in hysterics.

'Internet is the greatest worst thing'

Biden tripped over a sandbag while shaking hands with graduating cadets, sparking a viral moment on Instagram where he is now a meme. The long-time UFC commentator, Rogan shared the amusing meme on Instagram and said, "The internet is the greatest worst thing thatâ€™s ever happened." 

This is not the first time that Bidden fell. He was injured in December 2021 when he stumbled up the stairs of his plane but recovered and gave a salute. He also fell off a bicycle in June 2022 but was uninjured.

'Just doing your job, Joe'

After Rogan shared the meme on his official Instagram handle, his fans and followers started reacting to it. "Just doing your job, Joe," remarked aspiring UFC lightweight contender Matt Frevola. A fan commented on Rogan's post, referring to the original interview the UFC announcer conducted with Conor McGregor on the ground after he snapped his leg against Dustin Poirier at UFC 264, "YA WIFE IS IN ME DMS."

Dominick Cruz, a former UFC bantamweight champion and fellow commentator, chimed in with his observation, "No one can hold him down!" Cohutta Lee Grindstaff, 'The Real Worlds' contestant wrote, "It just me or does he have those customized Michael Jackson heels to get that Smooth Criminal lean?" Jonhy Walker, a martial artist mocked saying, "Always find a way to talk to the performers" with crying-laughing emojis.
