Who Is Brooklynn White? Her Age, Job

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 1, 2024

Brooklynn White is the girlfriend of Buster Murdaugh, the only surviving family member of disgraced attorney Alex Murdaugh. Buster Murdaugh, who has been dragged into the spotlight after the two homicides in his family, keeps himself away from the spotlight.

Richard Alexander “Buster” Murdaugh is the oldest of two sons of attorney Alex Murdaugh and his wife late Margaret (Maggie) Murdaugh. His father is currently on a trial for the murder of his brother and his mom in June 2021. Jurors were told by the prosecutor, Creighton Waters, how Murdaugh shot his kid with buckshot first before picking up an AR-15-style gun and killing his wife, Maggie, twice in the head. ‘It was a family weapon that killed Maggie Murdaugh,’ Creighton said.

Alex is currently facing trial for allegedly murdering his wife Maggie and younger kid Paul. Back on Buster’s girlfriend, are they married yet? What does she do for a living? Where is she from?

The answer unfolds as you scroll down this article.

Meet Brooklynn White, Buster Murdaugh Girlfriend

Fox News previously reported that Buster Murdaugh is reportedly “living a quiet life with his longtime girlfriend” and is based in Hilton Head Island, South Carolina. Buster has been dating Brooklynn White for a very long time. They live together in a modest $180,000 condo on Hilton Head Island.

One of the neighbors at the time called the couple a “really nice couple” who usually “smile and say hi.” In addition, a dog named Miller is said to belong to the courting couple. Buster does not currently have a wife because they are not yet married. There aren’t many details about their relationship.

The Greenville News brought into light the plethora of witnesses related to Alex’s trial and Buster was one of them. However, he had also been in legal trouble before that also involved his late brother and his dad.

A former college classmate of Buster revealed to People Magazine in July 2021 that the oldest son of Alex wasn’t doing well at all. They added, “He really withdrew after everything happened. Before, he’d text immediately. His phone was surgically implanted in his hand or something like you’d send a text 24/7 and he’d respond, and he loved to chat. Now he doesn’t respond to most texts or, if he does, it’s one or two words. He has really closed off and built walls around himself.”

But, he responded when DailyMail reached out. He explained, “Presumably you’re supporting your father?” he replied, “You have no right to presume anything.” And he was adamant as he said, “I don’t want to see it written anywhere that I’m supporting my father.”

When asked if he wanted to comment further on his father’s guilt or innocence, he simply answered, “I have no interest in saying anything. I have no comment.”

A childhood friend of his has also stated he’s “collateral damage” to his father’s alleged actions. “His circle of friends got really small really fast… He’s really down…,” the friend said. “He doesn’t make eye contact and he doesn’t really engage like he used to. He looks tired. He’s aged like 10 years in the past year. He has bags under his eyes and he just looks miserable. It’s just a shame.”

Brooklynn White Age

As of February 2023, Brooklyn White is 26 years old. She will turn 27 in mid-March 2023.

Brooklynn White Job

Brooklynn White is currently working as an attorney at Olivetti McCray & Withrow LLC. She joined the firm in the fall of 2021. Brooklynn was admitted to the bar of the State of South Carolina in 2022.

Her practice primarily focuses on estate planning, probate, and trust administration. Practicing law has always been her passion. She said in her bio, “I went to law school because I always had the desire to help people work through complex issues. I find it rewarding to help others.”

Brooklynn was a part of the Student Bar Association and the Assistant Research Editor for the Journal of Law and Education during her time in law school. She also took part in the Academic Success Program at the Law School, where she taught criminal law to first-year students. She also finished an externship with Judge Margaret B. Seymour of the U.S. District Court, for which she was awarded a Certificate of Excellence.

According to her LinkedIn, she interned at Elrod Pope Law Firm in 2017. She also served as a summer associate at Nelson Mullins Riley & Scarborough in 2019 and at Turner Padget Graham & Laney, P.A. in 2020. Moreover, she was a criminal law tutor at the University of South Carolina School of Law.

Brooklynn had also volunteered at UA Unified Sports, Rock Hill Elks Lodge, and Miracle League of Tuscaloosa.

Where Did Brooklynn White Acquire Her Education?

Brooklynn White acquired her law degree from the University of South Carolina School of Law in 2021. She received her undergraduate degree from the University of Alabama where she graduated Magna Cum Laude with a major in Political Science and a minor in Computer Science. During her time at Alabama, Brooklynn was involved in several student organizations.

Brooklynn completed her high school at Northwestern High School (Rock Hill, South Carolina).
