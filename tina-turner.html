Tina Turner's Biggest Fans

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 5, 2024

Media Platforms Design Team

The celeb quotient was high at Monday night's Tina Turner concert at Madison Square Garden. Guests included Michael Kors, Molly Sims, and Anne Hathaway, who shimmied unselfconsciously to "Simply the Best." Even Tina couldn't help but single out the biggest fan of the bunch; Tom Cruise, who was accompanied by wife Katie Holmes and three bodyguards, had floor seats. After egging him on to sing along to the encore, "Nutbush City Limits," she publicly thanked him while the capacity crowd applauded.
