Age, Biography and Wiki

Brian Deegan is an American professional freestyle motocross rider, racing driver, and a founding member of the Metal Mulisha. He is the first motocross rider to land a 360 in competition. He has won multiple X Games medals, including the first ever gold medal in the Moto X Best Trick event at the 2003 X Games. He has also competed in the Lucas Oil Off Road Racing Series, Monster Jam, and Rally America. Deegan has appeared in several films and television shows, including the MTV series Nitro Circus, the MTV movie Nitro Circus: The Movie, and the feature film Motocrossed. He has also appeared in several video games, including the Tony Hawk's Pro Skater series and the MX vs. ATV series. As of 2021, Brian Deegan's net worth is estimated to be roughly $2 million.
