Barrett Doss top movies, TV shows and awards

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Barrett Doss  is a singer and actress from the United States. Doss graduated from Manhattan’s Professional Children’s School. Doss attended the Gallatin School of Individualized Study at New York University.

Doss began performing off-Broadway in New York, and later on regional stages.

Barrett Doss top movies and TV shows

Barrett Doss starred in top movies and tv shows such as The Noel Diary, Marshall etc’

Barrett Doss  awards and nominations

Barrett Doss has won the Theatre World Award for Outstanding Broadway Debut Performance.

Doss rose to prominence as Victoria Hughes in the ABC action drama Station 19. Doss made her television debut in 2013, when she appeared in an episode of the NBC comedy series 30 Rock. Doss has appeared in several television shows, including Person of Interest, Bull, and Girls. Doss made her Broadway debut in the comedic play You Can’t Take It with You in 2014, co-starring with James Earl Jones and Rose Byrne.
