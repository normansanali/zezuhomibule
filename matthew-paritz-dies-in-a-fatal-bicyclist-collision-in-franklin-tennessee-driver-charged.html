Matthew Paritz Dies In a Fatal Bicyclist Collision in Franklin, Tennessee; Driver Charged

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 5, 2024

Franklin police have identified the 54-year-old cyclist, Matthew Paritz, who was fatally struck by an impaired driver. The incident occurred at Liberty Pike and Mack Hatcher Parkway in Franklin. Hidalgo Orozco Denilson, a 23-year-old from Washington, was arrested on charges of driving under the influence (DUI) and vehicular homicide. He is currently facing a substantial bail of $400,000 for his detention.

Following the tragic incident, Franklin police have initiated a thorough investigation to determine the exact circumstances leading to the collision. The investigation aims to gather crucial evidence and shed light on any potential contributing factors. Local authorities are committed to ensuring the safety of cyclists and motorists alike, and efforts will be intensified to analyze current traffic patterns and assess the need for improvements to prevent similar accidents in the future.

The passing of Matthew Paritz has deeply impacted the Franklin community, with local organizations and residents joining forces to extend support to the grieving family. Community leaders are considering organizing memorial events and fundraising initiatives to honor the memory of Matthew Paritz and raise awareness about road safety.

Hidalgo Orozco Denilson’s arrest on charges of DUI and vehicular homicide emphasizes the gravity of the incident. The legal system will now take its course to ensure justice is served for Matthew Paritz and his loved ones. The Franklin community eagerly awaits the outcome of this legal process, yearning for closure and justice in the wake of this devastating loss.
