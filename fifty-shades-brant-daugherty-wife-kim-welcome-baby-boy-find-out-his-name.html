Fifty Shades' Brant Daugherty &amp; Wife Kim Welcome Baby Boy - Find Out His Name!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, July 6, 2024

Brant Daugherty and his wife Kim Daugherty have welcomed their first child!

The couple announced their pregnancy a few months ago and revealed they were expecting a baby boy.

Click inside to see their newborn son’s name…

The couple announced the amazing news on both of their Instagram accounts. Brant, who you may recognize from the Fifty Shades franchise as well as Pretty Little Liars, shared the caption, “Wilder David Daugherty, meet the world.”

The couple chose the name Wilder David Daugherty for their newborn son. So cute!

Kim posted, “Welcome to the world, Wilder David Daugherty. You are so incredibly loved.”

If you missed it, Brant has an idea for a fourth Fifty Shades film!

Congratulations to the happy couple on the wonderful news!
