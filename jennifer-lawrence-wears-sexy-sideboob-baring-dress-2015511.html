Jennifer Lawrence Wears Sexy Sideboob-Baring Dress

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Sexy from the side! Jennifer Lawrence is on a skin-baring streak during this week’s The Hunger Games: Mockingjay – Part 2 premieres. After rocking a plunging Dior Haute Couture gown to the Berlin event on Wednesday, Nov. 4, she flaunted a different kind of cleavage in London the next day.

The Dior Haute Couture gown featured chains that held the front and back together so Lawrence’s entire torso — with plenty of sideboob — was exposed. Despite the low-cut side panels of Lawrence’s UK Dior Haute Couture ensemble, from the front, her frock looked like a flowing ball gown with red brushstroke-like dots in a flattering cut that created a waist-slimming effect.

The leading lady, 25, emphasized the romantic nature of the gown with moody makeup. “The Dior dress she was wearing was rich, and made for a sexy shot from the side, so we felt her makeup look should have the same feeling,” celeb makeup artist Jillian Dempsey said. To pick up the caramel flecks in the dress, Dempsey used Rouge Dior Lipstick #434 in Brun Smarcande. 

On the red carpet, Lawrence posed with her female costars, Game of Thrones‘ Gwendoline Christie, Natalie Dormer, Elizabeth Banks, and Julianne Moore, who also chose brooding formal looks for the occasion.
