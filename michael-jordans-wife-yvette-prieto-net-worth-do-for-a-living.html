Michael Jordans Model Wife Yvette Prieto Is Worth Millions, But Did She Make Any Of It Herself?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, July 26, 2024

Pro basketball player and business person, Michael Jordan, is an impressive individual who has accumulated an eye-watering net worth. He is well-known for his skills on the court, but he has also found happiness outside his work and his relationship with his second wife appears to be going strong. But who is his wife, Yvette Prieto? She is more than a pretty face and has also amassed a large fortune.

Fans of the couple may be wondering how her estimated net worth was calculated and how much of it did she have before marrying Jordan? While it is impossible to answer all of these questions, it is worth noting that Prieto had a career before she was the wife of an NBA star, and if they did ever split, she would certainly not be struggling for money…

How Long Have Michael Jordan And Yvette Prieto Been Married?

Does Michael Jordan need an introduction? Not really because he is one of the most celebrated professional basketball players in the world and also a successful businessperson.

But his career and business ventures are not the only thing he has enjoyed success with, he is also a father to five children and is in a long-term relationship with his wife, Yvette Prieto.

Yvette Prieto is Jordan's second wife (he was married to Juanita Vanoy from 1989 until 2006) and the couple have been together for a decade after marrying in a star-studded celebration in 2013. Jordan and Yvette Prieto are reported to have met in a nightclub in 2008, and it was not long before they moved in together, People Magazine reports.

In 2011, they got engaged, and two years later they were married. It was a big celebration that was held in Florida with 500 guests in attendance. There was also no expense spared to make this one of the most memorable days of their lives. Why? Because it is reported to have cost millions.

What Does Yvette Prieto Do, And What Is Her Net Worth?

We know that Juanita Vanoy and Michael Jordan divorced, and she is believed to have received a multi-million dollar settlement (via Hello! Magazine). But would the same happen if he and Yvette Prieto were to split? And what has she done before meeting Jordan that left her with such a large estimated worth?

Yvette Prieto worked as a model with some big names, including Alexander Wang, People Magazine reports. She is also a smart and established woman, having attended college for business management.

According to Parade, she had the role of manager for Beet the LBS, and property management firm Aqua Management, so she has proven she can handle roles in leadership. While it is clear that she can work on her own, her husband is mega-wealthy, and she is reported to have signed a prenup.
