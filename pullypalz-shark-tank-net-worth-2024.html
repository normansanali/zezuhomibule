The Pullypalz Concept

Pullypalz emerged as a creative solution to a common problem faced by parents: keeping pacifiers within an infant’s reach. The company’s products are designed with a pulley mechanism that allows babies to grab and pull one of two attached pacifiers, bringing it towards them. This not only solves the dropped pacifier issue but also encourages hand-eye coordination and fine motor skill development.
